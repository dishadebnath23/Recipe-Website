White Sauce Pasta

White sauce pasta is a creamy and indulgent pasta dish made with a rich white sauce as its base. The sauce, also known as béchamel sauce, is made by combining butter, flour, and milk, then seasoned with salt, pepper, and sometimes nutmeg. The cooked pasta is then tossed in this velvety sauce until evenly coated, creating a luscious and comforting dish. Optional additions like grated Parmesan cheese can be stirred in for extra flavor. White sauce pasta offers a versatile canvas for customization with ingredients like vegetables, chicken, or seafood, making it a beloved classic in Italian-inspired cuisine worldwide.

Here's a simple recipe for making White Sauce Pasta:

Ingredients:
- 250g pasta (any shape you prefer)
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 cups milk
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
- Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente (usually around 8-10 minutes).
    • Drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Prepare the White Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Once melted, add the flour and whisk continuously for about 1-2 minutes to cook the flour but not brown it.
  3. Add Milk:
    • Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  4. Season:
    • Add salt, black pepper, and nutmeg (if using). Stir well.
  5. Thicken:
    • Continue to cook the sauce, stirring frequently, until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Combine with Pasta:
    • Add the cooked pasta to the saucepan with the white sauce. Stir until the pasta is well coated.
  7. Add Cheese(optional):
    • If using Parmesan cheese, add it to the pasta and sauce mixture. Stir until the cheese is melted and well incorporated.
  8. Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh parsley or basil if desired.
    • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious white sauce pasta!
Feel free to customize the recipe by adding vegetables, chicken, or any other ingredients of your choice. Enjoy your meal!
